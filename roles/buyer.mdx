The **comprador** role is designed for coffee buyers ÔÇö cooperatives, processing mills, and independent traders ÔÇö who want to advertise their purchase prices to Colombian coffee farmers in the Huila region. Unlike producers, buyer accounts go through an admin approval step before they can publish prices or appear on the public map. This ensures that only legitimate, verified buyers are visible to the farming community.

<Warning>
  New buyer registrations **cannot publish prices or appear on the map** until an administrator has reviewed and approved the buyer profile. If your account is stuck in `pendiente`, check your email for a decision notification or contact the platform admin.
</Warning>

## Registration and Approval Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a user account">
    Register at `/register` and select **Comprador** as your role. Your user `estado` is set to `pendiente` immediately after registration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Enter the 6-digit code sent to your email to confirm your address. This step is required before you can proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your buyer profile">
    You are redirected to `/completar-perfil`. Fill in your company details (see [Profile Fields](#buyer-profile-fields) below). Submitting this form calls `POST /api/comprador` and creates your buyer record with `estadoRevision: "enRevision"`. An email notification is automatically sent to all active admins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for admin review">
    An admin reviews your profile. The `estadoRevision` moves from `enRevision` to either `aprobado` or `rechazado`. You receive an email with the decision.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    Once approved, your user `estado` becomes `activo` and you can access `/comprador/dashboard`, publish prices, and appear on the buyer map.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## estadoRevision Lifecycle

The `estadoRevision` field on the buyer record tracks where the profile is in the approval workflow:

```
[Registration] Ôćĺ enRevision Ôćĺ aprobado
                            Ôćĺ rechazado
perfilIncompleto (profile not yet submitted)
```

| estadoRevision | Meaning |
|----------------|---------|
| `perfilIncompleto` | Buyer account created but profile form not yet submitted |
| `enRevision` | Profile submitted, awaiting admin decision |
| `aprobado` | Admin approved ÔÇö buyer is visible on the map and can publish prices |
| `rechazado` | Admin rejected ÔÇö buyer cannot publish prices; a reason may be provided |

When a buyer is rejected, the `motivoRevision` field stores the reason. If the buyer resubmits or the issue is resolved, the admin can update `estadoRevision` back to `aprobado` via `PUT /api/comprador/:id`.

## What Approved Buyers Can Do

Once `estadoRevision` is `aprobado` and user `estado` is `activo`, buyers can:

- **Publish purchase prices** ÔÇö post the price they are currently offering per carga or per kg for specific coffee types.
- **Update or remove prices** ÔÇö keep their listed prices current throughout the trading day.
- **Appear on the regional map** ÔÇö their pin is shown on the public buyer map at `/mapa` with a general location.
- **Manage their company profile** ÔÇö update business details, schedule, and services via `PUT /api/comprador/:id`.
- **View their dashboard** ÔÇö access analytics and price management tools at `/comprador/dashboard`.

### Publishing Prices

`POST /api/precios` ÔÇö publish a purchase price for a specific coffee type.

```json
{
  "tipocafe": "pergamino_seco",
  "preciocarga": 1100000,
  "preciokg": 16923,
  "unidad": "carga"
}
```

Supported coffee types (`tipocafe`):

| Value | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `pergamino_seco` | Dry parchment coffee |
| `especial` | Specialty coffee |
| `organico` | Organic certified coffee |
| `verde` | Green (unroasted) coffee |
| `pasilla` | Lower-grade sorted coffee |
| `cacao` | Cacao (also traded on the platform) |
| `limon` | Lemon/citrus (complementary crop) |

Prices are denominated in **Colombian Pesos (COP)**.

## Privacy Model

Contact details are deliberately restricted to protect buyer privacy and maintain fair competition:

| Information | Producers & public | Buyer (own profile) | Admin |
|-------------|-------------------|---------------------|-------|
| Company name | Ôťů Visible | Ôťů Visible | Ôťů Visible |
| Municipality (general area) | Ôťů Visible | Ôťů Visible | Ôťů Visible |
| Exact address | ÔŁî Hidden | Ôťů Visible | Ôťů Visible |
| Phone number | ÔŁî Hidden | Ôťů Visible | Ôťů Visible |
| Business hours | Ôťů Visible | Ôťů Visible | Ôťů Visible |
| estadoRevision | ÔŁî Hidden | Ôťů Visible | Ôťů Visible |

The API response for unauthenticated users and producers uses `sanitizarCompradorPublico()`, which sets `direccion: null`, `telefono: null`, and `contactoRestringido: true`. Only the owner or an admin receives the full document.
